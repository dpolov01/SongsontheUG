About Songs on the Underground

Songs on the Underground is the brainchild of Daria, a life-long fan of all things tube-related.

It began with a conversation back in November 2016, and a subsequent appearance on K2K Radio to promote a fundraiser for Help Refugees. After appearing on Maha's Music, Daria was invited to host her very own radio show.

Two months later, in February 2017, Songs on the Underground was born - a radio show and podcast that takes a look at the fascinating history of London's tube network via a series of related songs.
